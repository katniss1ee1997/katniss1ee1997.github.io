About

I work with individuals who feel deeply but want to think clearly. My focus is on emotional structure and boundary awareness — understanding how patterns form, why they repeat, and how to regain stability without losing sensitivity.

This is not therapy. It is a structured emotional reflection with direction.

我服务于情绪敏锐、却希望保持清醒的人。咨询聚焦于情绪结构与边界觉察 —— 理解模式如何形成、为何重复，并在不压抑敏感的前提下重建稳定。

这不是心理治疗，而是有结构、有方向的情感思考。
